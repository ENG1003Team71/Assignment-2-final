How to use this app: Press RECORD to record a run . Press VIEW to view your runs
RECORD: Press Start to commence your run. Press stop upon completion. Time, distance and average speed can be viewed live during your run. Press refresh to reset the stopwatch to 0
VIEW: Press the play button corresponding to each run to view its details, including route, speed, time and distance ran. Press the bin button to delete a run
